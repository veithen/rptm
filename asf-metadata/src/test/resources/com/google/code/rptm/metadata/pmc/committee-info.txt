NOTE:
The data in this file appears in other venues.  Board resolutions (as
recorded in the board's minutes on the public web site) are required
to constitute these committees.  The PMC mailing lists (as recorded in
private on ezmlm) are the functional manifestation of these
committees.  Committee members adopt various handles in those mailing
lists that often differ from their apache.org handles.  Some PMC
maintain private and public record of a similar nature.  The
foundation maintains a private list of members and their project
associations.  Not all committee members utilize their @apache.org
email addresses.  Keeping these all in synch is difficult.  A best
effort is made by volunteers to maintain this file.

PMCs are responsible for keeping their portion of the records up-to-date, see:

http://www.apache.org/dev/pmc.html#newpmc

Change of PMC chair
===================
When updating PMC chairs, please ensure that the "chair" marker is changed in both the summary
and the detail sections. Also please ensure that the 'pmc-chairs' LDAP group is updated if necessary (*).

To list the contents of the 'pmc-chairs' group:
   ldapsearch cn=pmc-chairs # (this must be run on minotaur/people)
If the list is incorrect, please contact infrastructure via e-mail and/or JIRA to get it updated.

(*) i.e. unless both the outgoing and incoming chairs are chairs of another PMC.

When changing PMC group membership, please also change the associated LDAP committee group:
==================================

modify_committee.pl tlp -add=new_pmcmember
modify_committee.pl tlp -rm=ex_pmcmember

Also, please update the web-site file at:

https://svn.apache.org/repos/asf/infrastructure/site/trunk/xdocs/foundation/index.xml

and regenerate/deploy the site

** Please only use ASF e-mail addresses in section 1 **

Also, when using non-ASF e-mail addresses, please ensure that they are listed in
https://svn.apache.org/repos/private/committers/MailAlias.txt

2. REPORTING PROCESS AND SCHEDULE


The PMC and Board Committee Chairs are responsible for providing a
report to the Board on a quarterly basis, or on a as-requested basis
(new PMCs should send in a report each month for their first three
months).

Please submit your report with sufficient time to allow the board members
to review and digest. The very latest you should submit your report is
two full days prior to the board meeting. See the 'calendar.txt' file in
this directory for the Board's meeting schedule.

Your report should be sent in plain-text format to <board@apache.org>
with the word "report" and your project name in the subject so that
they can easily be searched/found. The content should also be committed
to the meeting agenda in the board directory in the foundation repository,
trying to keep a similar format to the others.

3. COMMITTEE MEMBERSHIP AND CHANGE PROCESS


The members of each PMC and Board Committee are detailed below.

NOTE:
    There is a required process which must be followed, in order to
    change the composition of a PMC. In summary, that process is:

    1) send mail to <board@apache.org> describing the change
    2) one of the Directors must acknowledge receipt of the change
    3) 72 hours after acknowledgement, the change is effective and
       this file may be updated to reflect the changes

    The resolution establishing this process was passed in December
    2002, and can be found in that meeting's minutes:

      http://www.apache.org/foundation/records/minutes/2002/board_minutes_2002_12_18.txt

    Note that Board and President's Committees cannot be changed
    by this method, but instead must be changed by Board Resolution
    or appropriate action by the President respectively, instead.
